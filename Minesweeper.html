Instructions

Use the left click button on the mouse to select a space on the grid. If you hit a Bunny, you lose.

You can right click a square with the mouse to place a carrot where you think a Bunny is. This allows you to avoid that spot.

The numbers on the board represent how many bombs are adjacent to a square. For example, if a square has a "3" on it, then there are 3 rabbits next to that square. The rabbits could be above, below, right left, or diagonal to the square.

Avoid all the bunnies and expose all the empty spaces to win Bunny Feeder.

Tip: Use the numbers to determine where you know a bomb is.
